Construction Services

Tanto Irrigation, LLC is dedicated to the preservation of golf through the professional installation of automated golf course irrigation systems. Tanto was founded on the core belief that if you provide quality workmanship at a fair price, news will travel fast. For over 50 years we have been providing just that for courses throughout the Northeast and the rest of the World. Tanto owns its own equipment and trains its own personnel. We travel only qualified employees who have been schooled on proper plumbing and electrical techniques. Tanto handles all installation types from PVC to HDPE, Satellite to 2-Wire, Toro to Rainbird, and everything else in between Our industry affiliations and National account status with major vendors bring all the advantages possible to your project.

Our Promise

Our business model is based on fundamental principles that provide for our consistent work product. The superintendent that begins your project will finish it. The essential part of his crew will remain the same through the life of your project. He will have well maintained equipment and the proper pieces for the job. He will have the administrative support necessary to complete his tasks in a timely and efficient manner. His crew size will be large enough to complete the work, but small enough to manage effectively. These principles are the foundation of the management philosophy that we adhere to on every project. Tanto never runs more than 6 crews domestically in order to maintain consistent quality control, which shortens both production times and punch lists. We truly are large enough to serve you, yet small enough to know you. You will be able to count on Tanto well after the contractual obligation has expired on your project. We treat each project with the respect and dignity that you as the designer, owner, superintendent, member, patron do and we hope that you would expect nothing less.
